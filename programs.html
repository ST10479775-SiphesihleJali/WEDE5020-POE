Our Programs

GreenSteps offers comprehensive programs to support community gardens from inception to sustainability. All our programs are free for registered community groups.

üå± Garden Establishment

We help communities identify suitable land, conduct professional soil tests, and establish productive gardens from the ground up. Our experienced team provides comprehensive site preparation, efficient irrigation setup, and initial planting guidance to ensure your garden thrives from day one.

Includes: Site assessment, soil testing, irrigation design, initial seedlings, and tools.

üë©‚Äçüåæ Skills Training Workshops

Monthly hands-on workshops covering organic composting methods, integrated pest management, seasonal planting calendars, and efficient harvesting techniques. All training is conducted in local languages by certified agricultural instructors with years of practical experience.

Topics: Composting, pest control, crop rotation, water management, seed saving, and organic certification.

üè´ School Garden Program

Educational gardens in schools teach children about nutrition, environmental science, and sustainable food production while providing fresh organic vegetables for school feeding schemes. We integrate garden activities into the curriculum to enhance learning across multiple subjects.

Benefits: Nutrition education, hands-on science learning, fresh produce for learners, and environmental awareness.

üíß Sustainability Initiatives

Advanced water conservation systems including rainwater harvesting, drip irrigation, and greywater recycling. We also provide renewable energy solutions such as solar pumps and implement comprehensive waste reduction programs to help gardens operate sustainably and minimize environmental impact.

Features: Rainwater tanks, solar irrigation, composting systems, and zero-waste practices.

üõí Market Access Program

We connect successful community gardens with local markets, restaurants, and distribution networks to create sustainable income opportunities for community members. Our program includes business skills training, quality control guidance, and ongoing market linkage support.

Services: Market connections, pricing guidance, packaging support, and entrepreneurship training.

üì± Digital Support Platform

Our mobile-friendly digital platform provides instant access to resources, seasonal planting alerts, weather updates, and virtual consultations with agricultural experts. Gardeners stay connected and informed throughout the entire growing season with 24/7 support.

Features: Mobile app, WhatsApp support groups, video tutorials, and online expert consultations.
